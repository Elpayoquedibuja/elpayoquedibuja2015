What is 'Kale Dor Kayiko'?

Kale Dor Kayiko is a is a gypsy educational association of The Basque Country.

It is founded 20 years ago.

His president is Uncle Manuel -El tío Manuel: Manuel Vizarraga-. Kale Dor Kayiko is the most important gypsy association of The Basque Country.

Kale Dor Kayiko
in 'Azokako Durango'

We were in 'Azokako Durango' The Fair of the book and the music of "Durango". It is the first time that gypsy association of The Basque Country announce.

Kale Dor Kayiko with 'The gypsy Queen' in the Azokako Durango.

The gypsy Queen, Rosario Montoya, is pianist and composer.

She was the first Gipsy woman in this country obtaining the qualification of Titulo de Profesora e Instrumentista de Piano (Pianist and Piano Instructor). She is a young star of the Flamenco.
